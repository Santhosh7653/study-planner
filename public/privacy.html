Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026  ·  Study Planner

1. What We Collect

When you use Study Planner, we collect the following information:

  • Your email address and display name (via Google sign-in or email/password registration)
  • Tasks you create, including titles, deadlines, priorities, and notes
  • Google Calendar OAuth tokens, used solely to sync your tasks with your calendar

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the Study Planner service:

  • Your email and name identify your account and personalise your experience
  • Your tasks are stored in Firebase Firestore to enable cross-device access
  • Your Google Calendar access token is used only to create, update, and delete calendar events that correspond to your tasks

3. Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore, protected by Firebase Security Rules that ensure only you can read or write your own data. We do not store your Google account password.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. The only external service your data touches is Google (Firebase and Google Calendar), which is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Google Calendar Access

Study Planner requests access to your Google Calendar solely to sync your study tasks. We request the minimum required scope (calendar.events) and never read, modify, or delete calendar events that were not created by this app.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete individual tasks at any time within the app. To request full account deletion, contact us at the email below.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out at support@studyplanner.app.
